Sobre Mí

Hi, if you have arrived here, that means that you want to know more, I'm also a curious person, you are about to discover it.

To start I will comment the art works that have influenced me most: games like Zelda or Fallout, movies like Clerks or Princess Mononoke, series like Dragonball or Futurama, Books like hitchhiker's guide of the galaxy or At the mountains of madness and bands like LPR or Ramones. So that will get you an idea.

I like to define myself as a restless person, who experiences and investigates everything that catches my attention. You never know anything until you learn it. I consider myself a creative person and that is why I have focused my career in software development, in this last stage I am focusing on the art of creating video games, which for me is the best way to tell a story in the 21 century. That's why I'm now collaborating to create Drop at Maturuturu Studios.

In short, I am a simple guy looking for his place, welcome to my world!

EasyDraw

EasyDraw is my simple drawing tool. it can create, save and retrieve designs we do with this application. The interface is comfortable and friendly. Furthermore, EasyDraw has a command console with which you can make your creations with the keyboard, without using the mouse. EasyDraw is also a tool that uses parameterization of objects, to which we can apply symmetries, rotations or scale them. Designed entirely in Visual Basic.

Download EasyDraw

CheesMadness

CheesMadnes is a chess game designed for two players. As long as you use the mouse, the other can use the keyboard. This app shows the departure time, we corrected the erroneous movements and warn us when checkmate is made. On the other hand also allows us to save the game at all times. Designed entirely in Visual Basic.

Download CheesMadness

Su-Doku

Su-Doku, our game Sudoku with different levels of difficulty (Easy-Medium-Hard). The game incorporates a random Sudokus to be solved. We may check at any time if we have provided an incorrect number and if we can not solve the game will take care of us. We can play Su-Doku both the mouse and the keyboard. Designed entirely in Visual Basic.

Download Su-Doku
